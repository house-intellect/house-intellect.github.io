NFC Check List: Reports and Maintenance Manuals

Control your subordinates via Google Form instructions associated with NFC tags!

Scanning NFC tags attached to equipment shows relevant Google Form Surveys to your service personnel. Those checklists contain maintenance intstruction manuals and job descriptions relevant to the specific set of equipment marked by the NFC tag. The association between NFC tags and Google Forms URLs is established by using NFC Reports app linking NFC tags with corresponding calendar maintenance events. Maintenance supervisor creates these events in Google Calendar and places Google Forms Surveys URLs into their description fields. NFC Reports app creates a calendar shared with the service personnel accounts using NFC Check List app. Automatic delivery of relevant maintenance manuals to technicians makes it possible to replace business critical executives with less expenses, increases job reports transparency and introduces an inventory tracking solution. Reports and Instruction Manuals are to be implemented in the existing Google Forms database of the company.

To link an NFC tag to a Google Form Survey

  1. Create a Google Form in your Google Drive
  2. Create a shortened URL for the created Form by pressing the “Send” button
  3. Create a new event in your “NFC” Google Calendar, this calendar is created by the app during the first launch
  4. Paste your Form URL into the description field of the new calendar maintenance event
  5. Open the NFC Reports app and create a new NFC tag by scanning it
  6. Choose the calendar event you created previously from the offered events list in the NFC tag editor dialog
  7. Add the subordinate technician’s account to the access list in “Users” tab
  8. Install NFC Check List on the subordinate technician’s smartphone
  9. Scan the tag with the NFC Check List and see the Google Form Survey being offered for filling out and submitting
