IoT Battery Charger for Android


NFC task list on Google Play

This app intelligently manages your phone’s charging cycle using your existing Google Home compatible smart switch. Simply connect your phone charger to a smart switch, select the switch within the app, and set your desired charging window (e.g., start charging at 20%, stop at 80%).

Key Features:

  • Google Home Integration: Seamlessly connects to your Google Home ecosystem to control your smart switches.
  • Customizable Charging Limits: Set specific lower and upper battery percentage thresholds (hysteresis) to start and stop charging.
  • Automatic Charging Control: The app monitors your phone’s battery level and automatically turns your smart switch ON or OFF.
  • Battery Health Protection: Prevents your battery from staying at 100% for extended periods or dropping too low, which can help extend its overall lifespan.
  • Peace of Mind: Charge your phone overnight without anxiety.
  • Ideal for Always-On Devices: Perfect for phones used as dedicated Wi-Fi hotspots, security cameras, or information displays that are constantly plugged in.
  • Current Charge Level Display: See your phone’s battery percentage directly in the app.
  • Auto Maintain Charge Toggle: Easily enable or disable the automatic charging management.
  • Dark & Light Theme: Choose the theme that suits your preference.

How It Works:

  1. Ensure your phone charger is plugged into a Google Home compatible smart switch.
  2. Open IoT Battery Charger and grant necessary permissions.
  3. Select your smart switch from the list of available Google Home devices.
  4. Set your preferred Lower Limit (e.g., 20%) and Upper Limit (e.g., 80%) for charging.
  5. Toggle “Auto Maintain Charge” ON.
    The app will now monitor your battery. When it drops below the Lower Limit, the switch will turn ON. When it reaches the Upper Limit, the switch will turn OFF.

Requirements:

  • An Android phone.
  • Google Home app installed and set up.
  • A Google Home compatible smart switch (e.g., smart plug).
  • Active internet connection for Google Home communication.

Download IoT Battery Charger today and take control of your phone’s charging for better battery longevity!
